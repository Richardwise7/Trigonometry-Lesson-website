Trigonometry Quiz

Test your knowledge of trigonometric concepts with the following questions. Choose the correct answer for each question.

1. What is sin 30°?

a) 1
b) √3/2
c) 1/2
d) √2/2

2. Identity: 1 + tan²θ = ?

a) sin²θ
b) sec²θ
c) cos²θ
d) csc²θ

3. The value of cos 60° is:

a) 0
b) 1/2
c) √3/2
d) 1

4. If sin A = 3/5, then cos A equals:

a) 4/5
b) 5/4
c) 1/5
d) 2/5

5. Which identity is correct?

a) 1 + cot²θ = cos²θ
b) 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
c) 1 + sin²θ = cos²θ
d) tan²θ = sin²θ + cos²θ

6. sin²θ + cos²θ is equal to:

a) 0
b) 1
c) sin θ
d) cos θ

7. The value of tan 45° is:

a) 0
b) 1
c) √3
d) √2

8. Simplify (1 − cos²θ):

a) sin²θ
b) cos²θ
c) tan²θ
d) sec²θ

9. Which of the following is equal to sin 2θ?

a) 2 sin θ cos θ
b) cos²θ − sin²θ
c) tan θ
d) sec²θ

10. If tan θ = 1, then θ equals:

a) 0°
b) 30°
c) 45°
d) 90°
